Pharmacy Identification Verification System

Pharmacy Identification Verification System

A unique platform to verify national identification number assigned by the National Council for collecting data from pharmacies

Highlights

This project was owned by US Client NCPDP (National Council for National Council for Prescription Drug Programs) it is non-profit organization, setting standard and data exchange between pharmacies identity data.

They use this project, for collecting data from pharmacies, their complete information and license details are maintained. Each and every field is run through the approval workflow.

Service Provided: we have started this project for support and maintenance. After 1 year of support and simple enhancement. We had got the order for upgrade to latest tech stack .

  • .Net framework 4
  • ASP.NET with MVC framework
  • Workflow Management by using the Microsoft Workflow.
  • Database was MSSQL, 2016

Technologies Used: MySQL, classic ASP.net, .Net 1.0,JavaScript, jQuery, Bootstrap, HTML/CSS development, GitLab

Business domain: Healthcare Industry

Complete migration was completed in 6 month timeframe. With team of 5 developers.
