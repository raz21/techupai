About Us

We Create Designs and Technology

TECHUPAI delivers enterprise mobility solutions for SMEs, Start-ups, and full-grown enterprises across the world. Since inception, the focal point of our business has been to create profound value for our customers by crafting intelligent solutions and services in path-breaking and cost-effective ways.

We provide cutting-edge platforms in the field of Data Analytics, Cloud services, Analytics, QA, Mobile App, Custom Software Development, web development, E-commerce solutions, Digital marketing and Consulting Services for several business verticals like Retail, Education, Healthcare, IT, Finance, Manufacturing, and Insurance among others.

We believe in developing and maintaining long term relationships with our clients and endeavor to provide best services in order to reap maximum benefits. Quality and commitment is something everyone looks for and that's what we strive to deliver in whatever we do. Client satisfaction is the foremost objective of our services.
